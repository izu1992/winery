Chateau Napareuli Boutique Hotel enjoys a quiet location among the wineyards of Kakhetii - Georgia’s primewine-producing area sited in the close proximty of the Greater Caucasus. It is well connected with a must-see regional spots, such as Gremi fortress (12km) or Alaverdi Monastery (20km) and a world-famous Abano Pass (within a distance of 40 km) which is high mountain pass at the elevation of 2850 meters above the sea level - the greatest European road available for 4x4 cars leading alongside magnificent region of Tusheti. The visitors will have a unique chance to experience Tusheti region with a jeep adventure tours offered by the Chateau Napareuli. Chateau also provides the visitors with an opportunity to examinea great selection of natural, home-made, traditional Georgianwinesand local culinary delights. Chateau Napareuli is looking forward to treating the guests to culiary delights from local region is the restaurant offering a mountain-view terrace. The distance to Tbilisi (100 km) can be reached either by public (bus) or private transportation offered by the hotel.
