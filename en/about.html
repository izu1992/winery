Chateau Napareuli is located in the village of Napareuli, in the Kakheti region, 100 km from the capital of Georgia - Tbilisi.
Our house was built in 1917. In 2017 we have started his renovation, restoring its original character - kakhetian house producing wine.
Napareuli's appeal is located in the heart of the Alazanii Valley, the cultivars in it are: rkatsiteli, saperavi, mtsvane, kisi, khikhvi.
The heart of the house is marani, the place where the wine arises and where it is stored.
At Chateau Napareuli, we produce wine in kvevri, according to the old georgian recipe.
